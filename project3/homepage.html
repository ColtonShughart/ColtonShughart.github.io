WELCOME TO WONDERLAND

"Alice's Adventures in Wonderland," written by Lewis Carroll, was first published in 1865. He completed the manuscript in 1864 and sought an illustrator, eventually collaborating with John Tenniel, whose iconic illustrations became synonymous with the book.

The book was published by Macmillan & Co. in 1865, and it quickly gained popularity for its imaginative narrative and playful language. Carroll's unique style and the book's enduring themes of curiosity and identity have made "Alice's Adventures in Wonderland" a beloved classic in children's literature.
