Step 8

Using your pen of choice, outline the outer shape of Red, his eyes and beak. Then erase the framing lines from step 2. The last thing to do is color. Red is the main color, dark red is for the extra dots, black for the pupils and eyebrows, peach for the belly, yellow for the top beak, and yellow-orange for the bottom beak.
